Using AI Effectively

Jochen Voss, August 2025.

  • Slides
  • Background

This covers practical approaches to using AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) effectively in research and professional work. The materials explore topics including getting better results from tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and others, understanding technical concepts like tokens and context windows, and avoiding common pitfalls such as hallucinations and security issues. The focus is on real-world applications and what actually works rather than on limitations.

AI was used heavily in preparing these materials.
